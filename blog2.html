Mental Health Crisis: A Silent Emergency Among Youth

By SKR | July 2, 2025

Introduction

Hum sab kuchh na kuchh jhel rahe hain — pressure of career, competition, heartbreak, loneliness. Par sabse khatarnak hai — mental health ko ignore karna. Ye blog un logon ke liye hai jo haste to hain, par andar se tut chuke hain.

The Unseen Battle

Depression, anxiety, burnout — ye sab sirf "bigde hue logon" ke issues nahi hain. UPSC aspirants, college students, job seekers — sab is se guzarte hain. Mental illness kisi ko bhi ho sakti hai, aur sabko support ki zarurat hoti hai.

Why Youth Are Suffering More

  • Social media pressure – sab perfect dikhte hain, par reality alag hai
  • Family expectations – “IAS banna hai”, “Shaadi kab karega?”, “Marks kyun kam hain?”
  • Relationship trauma – pyaar to sabko hota hai, par heartbreak sabko sambhal nahi aata
  • Isolation – doston ke beech bhi tanha lagta hai

Signs You Shouldn’t Ignore

  • Neend na aana ya zyada sona
  • Khaane ka mann na karna
  • Sabse alag rehna
  • Suicidal thoughts – “khatam kar doon sab”

Toh please kisi trusted friend ya mental health expert se baat karo.

What Can You Do?

  • Apne emotions ko jaano – journal likho
  • Professional help lo – therapy sharam ki baat nahi hai
  • Break lo – har waqt productive rehna zaruri nahi
  • Apne liye raho – self-love zaruri hai
  • Friend se sirf "kaisa hai" poochhna bhi kaafi hota hai

You're Not Alone ❤

"You don't have to control your thoughts. You just have to stop letting them control you." – Dan Millman
