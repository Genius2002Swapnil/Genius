📘 UPPSC Exam Pattern

📝 Prelims

  • 2 Objective Papers – General Studies & CSAT
  • Each paper carries 200 marks
  • CSAT is qualifying (33% minimum required)

📚 Mains

  • Total 8 papers – Subjective (Descriptive)
  • Includes General Hindi, Essay, GS Papers & Optional Subject
  • Each GS & Optional paper: 200 marks

🎙 Interview

  • Personality Test – 100 Marks
  • DAF based questions – Focus on background, interests, current issues
🔔 Note: Final merit is based on Mains + Interview only. Prelims is qualifying in nature.
